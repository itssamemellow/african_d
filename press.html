Updates

African Diamond Nominated for 2025 Top 16 Youth-Owned Brands Awards

African Diamond, the proudly youth-owned luxury wine brand, has been nominated for the 2025 Top 16 Youth-Owned Brands Awards hosted by Pat on Brands. This recognition celebrates African Diamond's commitment to excellence, innovation, and African heritage.

The awards will take place on 21 June 2025 in Sandton, Johannesburg.

Behind the Wine

Wine at the Brewery Down South

On August 21, 2025, the African Wine Bar hosted its flagship event, Wine Down at the Brewery, at Dirty South Brewery in Margate. Curated by founder Tandokuhle Mpinda, the evening paired hearty platters with a guided tasting of YBY Crystal Dry, Montpellier Spyseniersberg Chenin Blanc, Essay Red Blend, and Montpellier Spyseniersberg Red. The event reinforced the African Wine Bar’s role in shaping memorable wine experiences.

African Wine Bar Delivers at GL.O.W.I Wine Society Seasonal Dining Experience

On June 28 2025, African Wine Bar brought elegance, education, and African wine culture to life at GL.O.W.i.'s seasonal dining event at Momo Kuro, Rosebank. The experience featured expert wine pairings, live performances, and a powerful showcase of boutique Southern African wines.

African Wine Bar X Eighteen 13: Chef Vee Gala Dinner Takeover

The African Wine Bar, in collaboration with Eighteen13, was proud to provide the official wine service at Chef Vee's exclusive Gala Dinner on the 3rd of May 2025—a refined evening of fine dining, laughter, and expertly paired wines.

MOTE "Monkey On The Elephant"

African Diamond Wines proudly participated in the grand relaunch of Wine Wednesday at MOTE in Fourways on March 26, 2025. Guests indulged in our signature Grenache Noir and Grenache Blanc, with many making the switch to The Billionaire's Choice. The evening featured live performances, including a set by DJ Tira, making it a night to remember.

Airport Company South Africa Presents: Wine and Art Festival

African Diamond Wines was honoured to be invited to showcase at the first instalment of the Wine and Art Festival hosted by Airports Company South Africa at King Shaka International Airport on the 1st and 2nd of March 2025.

African Diamond Wines & African Wine Circle Presents: Malcolm X Day

On February 21, 2025, African Diamond Wines and the African Wine Circle hosted an elegant evening in Rosebank, celebrating Malcolm X's legacy with fine wine, African cuisine, and inspiring discussions.
