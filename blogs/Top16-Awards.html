African Diamond Shines Bright as 2025 Top 16 Youth-Owned Brands Awards Nominee

Johannesburg, South Africa - African Diamond, a proudly youth-owned luxury wine brand rooted in African elegance and innovation, has been officially nominated for the prestigious Top 16 Youth-Owned Brands Awards (YOBAs) 2025.

Organized by Pat on Brands, the YOBAs recognize and celebrate trailblazing youth-owned brands across the continent who are redefining excellence, purpose, and cultural pride. This nomination places African Diamond among Africa's most dynamic and inspiring businesses.

“We are incredibly honored to be acknowledged in this way,” says Tandokuhle Mpinda, founder of African Diamond. “This nomination speaks to the vision and hard work of our team, and the continued support of our loyal customers. African Diamond is more than a wine label—it's a story of refinement, heritage, and youth potential.”

As a brand born out of a passion for excellence and storytelling, African Diamond represents a new generation of African luxury. From its signature Grenache Noir and Grenache Blanc wines to its curated lifestyle experiences, the brand continues to bridge tradition and modern elegance, inspiring pride across borders.

There are incredible things coming from African Diamond in the months ahead. New experiences. New milestones. And more opportunities to celebrate African excellence. Stay tuned - the journey has just begun.
