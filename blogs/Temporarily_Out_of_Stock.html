Temporary Pause, Same Great Taste

Explore Our Sister Brand!

First and foremost we'd love to commend you on your enthusiasm and receiving our wine with open arms, the reception has been nothing short of humbling. We appreciate the support and eagerness you've shown to African Diamond Wines. Most if not all of our clients are repeat buyers and that speaks to the quality and perfection of our wines.

With that being said, We're currently experiencing a high demand for our wines, and for this reason it is temporarily out of stock, We are working to restock and bottle our inventory and it will be available again soon we promise.

However, fret not for we have an alternative solution to your wine woes, African Diamond Wines in Collaboration with its sister brand The African Wine Bar are still very much available. The African Wine Bar has a selection of hand picked bespoke South African boutique wines that are equally amazing and will leave your guests mesmerised.

African Wine Bar services events, parties, gala dinners and exhibitions, do not hesitate to enquire further if this sounds like just what you and your event need. We invite you to book our experience via our website.

Click here to book or Enquire at info@theafricandiamond.co.za

Until then, African Diamond Wines will be back as your favourite everyday wine, in your highs and in your lows we will be there.
