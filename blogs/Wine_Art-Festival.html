Airport Company South Africa: Presents Wine and Art Festival

King Shaka International Airport

African Diamond Wines was honoured to be invited to showcase at the first instalment of the Wine and Art Festival, hosted by Airports Company South Africa, at King Shaka International Airport on 1 and 2 March, 2025.

This two-day event shone a spotlight on small, up-and-coming businesses such as wine owners and artists in all their forms and proffered them with exposure to a market they otherwise wouldn't have had access to. These are the kind of initiatives and opportunities that ACSA and the government are creating for Proudly South African Youth in Business.

From engaging business conversations to inspiring artistic showcases, the festival brought together industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and creatives for a memorable experience.

Attendees had the opportunity to explore ACSA's business opportunities, network with key decision-makers from CAPEX Infrastructure, Property, Retail and Commercial in addition to connecting with visionary, young designers and artists whose work captures the essence of South Africa.

Proudly South African winemakers such as The African Diamond showcased their finest selections, while a stellar lineup of artists -including Shekhinah, DJ Sox, DJ Sonic and more- set the perfect ambience.

African Diamond Wines showed up and showed off their vintage selection, namely, the Grenache Blanc and Grenache Noir. Guests were left speechless after being offered a taste test, often citing they'd never tasted anything like it and wanting more.

The Wine and Art Festival was a success and African Diamond Wines simply cannot wait for the following one.
