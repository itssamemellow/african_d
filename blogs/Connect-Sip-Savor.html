Connect, Sip, Savor and Save the Date: 27 March 2026

You, Me and a Bottle of Wine

Mark your calendars for an evening where wine, art, fashion, and connection meet in perfect harmony. On March 27, African Wine Circle collaborates with African Diamond Premium Wines for “You, Me and a Bottle of Wine”, a curated lifestyle experience celebrating culture, elegance, and meaningful social moments.

This time the African Wine Bar travels to Abuja to provide an experience that offers a journey through premium African wines paired with exquisite canapés, with each sip and bite designed to delight the senses. The atmosphere is elevated by art installations and thoughtful touches that reflect African creativity, providing the perfect backdrop for conversation, connection, and shared moments. Guests are encouraged to embrace Africa Flair fashion, bringing bold prints, refined silhouettes, and personal style to the night. Every element, from the wines to the canapés to the curated social spaces, is crafted to inspire interaction, enjoyment, and memorable experiences. African Diamond Premium Wines anchors the evening with selections designed for passion, ambition, and milestone moments, while African Wine Circle continues its mission of building wine culture and celebrating lifestyle across the continent.

Save the date: 27 March 2026. Dress with intention, arrive ready to connect, and let the wine, art, and fashion guide the evening. You Me and a Glass of Wine is a night designed to be savoured.
