26 March 2025

MOTE “Monkey On The Elephant”

Forest Rd & Sunset Ave, Fourways

African Diamond Wines treated guests to a beautiful wine tasting at Wine Wednesday's much anticipated relaunch at MOTE “Monkey On The Elephant” in Fourways on the 26th of March 2025.

Wine Wednesday made its coveted return in style, sparing no expenses to give guests nothing but the very best. So of course African Diamond Wine was in the building, as the Billionaires choice nothing else made sense.

Guests came flocking in and marvelled at the beauty of both our Grenache Noir and Grenache Blanc, siting that they've never tasted anything quite like it, many saying they've been “converted” and will be making the switch to African Diamond, and that is exactly what they did!. Many made African Diamond Wines their drink of choice for the rest of the evening and stocked for home.

Wine Wednesday is a great event that celebrates wine and creates a space for wine lovers to connect and be exposed to local and black owned wine brands that they otherwise wouldn't have known about. The event is exactly the type of event African Diamond Wines want to be affiliated with, an event that uplifts and opens up space because we believe there is enough room in the table for all of us.

As the rest of the evening progressed, live performances from well know DJs and performers commenced and the likes of DJ Tira were among them.

African Diamond Wines is looking forward to collaborating with Wine Wednesday again in the near future.
