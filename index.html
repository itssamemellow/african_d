Grenache Noir

Sourced from the rare, dryland bush vine vineyards, our Grenache Noir exemplifies the intense concentration of flavors that these resilient, low-yield vines produce.

— Read More

Grenache Blanc

Fermented with wild yeasts in 300-liter French oak barrels, our Grenache Blanc develops rich and complex characters with subtle notes of vanilla and spice.

— Read More

Swartland Region

The Swartland region, with its rich history of viticulture, is renowned for its ability to produce high-quality grapes. One of the distinctive features of this region is its dryland bush vine vineyards, which thrive in the clay soils.
