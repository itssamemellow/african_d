African Diamond

We as The African Diamond family pride ourselves with ensuring that everything that comes from us represents who we are.

As a brand, we value pristine quality, refined excellence, and exclusivity. This ensures we maintain our integrity and stay true to our roots as creators of a timeless legacy of perfection.

Our Story

After our quest throughout Africa for the perfect location to source our grapes and begin the manufacturing process, we settled on South Africa.

Given the country's overwhelming variety of grapes and wine we were faced with the arduous -albeit enjoyable- task of finding the perfect grapes for each African Diamond wine. Our dream has always been to introduce a wine of pre-eminence, to be shared on special occasions with those dearest to you. Our wines have far-reaching impact to interconnect and cultivate connections not only for those within Africa, but further into the rest of the world.

Vision

Introducing a carefully-crafted, exclusive wine with class. Presenting delectable quality in a bottle so delightful it unlocks important notes, memories and represents momentous milestones with every sip.

Appreciate the pure velvety tickle on the palate infused with rich leathery notes and aromas. A wine which speaks to those who have ambition and passion to succeed. Enjoy The Billionaire's Choice.
