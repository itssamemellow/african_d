Shipping & Delivery

At The African Diamond, we strive to ensure that your order reaches you in pristine condition and within the agreed delivery times.

Delivery

Standard delivery is 3-5 business days within South Africa.

Express delivery available apon request, with delivery in 1-2 business days. Additional charges apply.

We ship globally! Delivery time may vary between 7-14 business days, depending on the destination country.

Delivery Fees:

Local Rate: R60 for orders within Johannesburg.

Domestic Orders: Flat rate of R80 for standard delivery and R100 for express delivery.

International Orders: Fees are calculated dynamically based on the weight of your order and the shipping destination.

Tracking Your Order:

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number, allowing you to monitor your shipment in real-time. We will send you an email once your order has been shipped.

Lost or Damaged Shipments:

In the rare event that your order is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We will promptly arrange for a replacement or refund where needed.
