Past Events

4 October 2025

Food Is Art Brunch: A Feast for the Senses

The Old Orchard

The Food Is Art Brunch returned this past weekend at The Old Orchard, offering guests a multisensory celebration of food, wine, and creativity. With panoramic mountain views as the backdrop, the sold-out event brought together fine dining, soulful music, and South African excellence.

Curated by Viwe, founder of Food Is Art, and paired by Tandokuhle, CEO of African Wine Bar, guests enjoyed a seven-course journey with boutique wines. Highlights included garlic butter lamb chops with Montpellier Spyseniersberg Red and a poached pear finale with YBY Sparkling.

African Wine Bar’s barmen added theatre to every pour, while live music filled the air, sparking dancing and joyful connections. By the end, guests were calling for more, many leaving with bottles in hand as a lasting memory of the experience.

21 August 2025

Wine at the Brewery Down South

Corner Valley & National Road, Margate

On August 21, 2025, the African Wine Bar hosted its flagship event, Wine Down at the Brewery, at Dirty South Brewery in Margate. Curated by founder Tandokuhle Mpinda, the evening paired hearty platters with a guided tasting of YBY Crystal Dry, Montpellier Spyseniersberg Chenin Blanc, Essay Red Blend, and Montpellier Spyseniersberg Red.

Blending food, culture, and connection, the event created a warm, communal atmosphere that reflected the African Wine Bar’s vision of making wine experiences approachable and memorable on the South Coast.

21 February 2025

Malcolm X Commemoration

Home Suite Hotels, Rosebank

The African Diamond and The African Wine Circle hosted a powerful evening commemorating the 60th anniversary of Malcolm X's passing. This Pan-African gathering brought together guests from across the continent to celebrate his legacy of activism, unity, and empowerment.

The evening featured a curated wine experience, soul-stirring live African music, and engaging discussions, all set against an atmosphere of cultural appreciation. Guests were welcomed with African Diamond's signature wines and treated to a Black Scottish massage station, adding a unique touch to the occasion. A lively quiz competition challenged guests' knowledge of African history, sparking meaningful connections and thought-provoking discussions.

The evening's curated wine and canapé experience served as a tribute to Africa's rich cultural heritage, offering a taste of its diverse traditions. Read more about the event.

1 February 2025

Latin Rhythms & Fine Wine

Rocky Ridge Bar & Music Venue | Ramsgate Beach, Margate

Rocky Ridge came alive with the vibrant sounds of The Hat Trick, setting the perfect atmosphere for an unforgettable Latino evening. Guests indulged in a curated dining experience by Fika Deli & Café, featuring authentic Latin flavors, from rich and smoky Birria beef stew to tender Mexican Chicken Adobo—all paired to perfection with African Diamond's exquisite wines.
